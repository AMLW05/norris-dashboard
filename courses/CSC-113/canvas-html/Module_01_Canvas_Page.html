üí° Drew's Tips for Success

  • Start early: Don't wait until Week 2 to begin. Spread work across both weeks.
  • Commit frequently: Small, frequent commits are better than one giant commit at the end.
  • Use good commit messages: This is 10% of your process grade! Formula: [Action Verb] + [What Changed]
  • Ask questions early: Discord #module-01 channel and office hours are there for you.
  • Follow Sacred Flow exactly: Every step matters. Don't skip the Issue or PR!

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Pitfalls to Avoid

  • Committing directly to main: Always create a feature branch first!
  • Vague commit messages: "update" tells me nothing. Be specific!
  • Waiting until the last day: GitHub has a learning curve. Give yourself time.
  • Not reading instructions: Skimming leads to missing requirements. Read completely.
  • Forgetting to link PR to Issue: Add "Closes #X" in your PR description.
