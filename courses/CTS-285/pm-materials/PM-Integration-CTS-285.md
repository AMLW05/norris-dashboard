## PM Learning Outcomes for CTS-285

Building on CSC-113 (basic sprints) and CSC-114 (Scrum fundamentals), CTS-285 adds:

1. **Agile Mindset**: Understand Agile values and principles, not just practices
2. **Stakeholder Management**: Identify stakeholders, gather requirements, manage expectations
3. **Risk Management**: Identify risks, assess impact, create mitigation plans
4. **Release Planning**: Plan multiple sprints, manage epics, prioritize features
5. **MVP Thinking**: Define minimum viable product, iterative delivery

#### 4. **Risk Management**
**When**: Module 4 (throughout project)
**Concept**: What could go wrong? How do we respond?
**Activity**: Risk brainstorming for AlgoCratic scenarios
**Application**: Create and maintain risk register

**Risk Register Template**:
| Risk | Probability | Impact | Score | Response | Owner |
|------|-------------|--------|-------|----------|-------|
| API rate limits hit | Medium | High | 15 | Implement caching | Me |
| Scope creep | High | Medium | 15 | Use change control | Me |
| Third-party API changes | Low | High | 10 | Version pinning, monitoring | Me |

**Risk Response Strategies**:
- **Avoid**: Change plan to eliminate risk
- **Mitigate**: Reduce probability or impact
- **Transfer**: Use service/insurance to handle
- **Accept**: Acknowledge, have contingency plan

**Mini-Lesson**: "Risk Management for Tech Projects" (15 min)

#### 6. **MoSCoW Prioritization**
**When**: Throughout course
**Concept**: Ruthless prioritization of features

**MoSCoW Method**:
- **Must Have**: Non-negotiable, project fails without it
- **Should Have**: Important but not critical
- **Could Have**: Nice to have if time permits
- **Won't Have** (this time): Explicitly out of scope

**Example for AI Tutor**:
- **Must**: Basic chat, AI responses, conversation history
- **Should**: Export chat, save favorites, customization
- **Could**: Voice input, multilingual, analytics
- **Won't**: Video chat, AR/VR interface

**Activity**: MoSCoW your AlgoCratic and capstone backlogs

## Assessment Integration

### Process Grading (30% of project grade)
- **Agile Mindset**: Evidence of iteration, adaptation (5%)
- **Stakeholder Management**: Communication quality, feedback incorporation (5%)
- **Risk Management**: Risk register maintained, proactive mitigation (5%)
- **Sprint Quality**: Planning, execution, retrospectives (10%)
- **Release Planning**: Roadmap updates, MVP focus (5%)

### Required PM Artifacts
1. Stakeholder register (updated monthly)
2. Risk register (updated each sprint)
3. Epic/theme organization (backlog structure)
4. MVP canvas
5. 3-sprint release roadmap (updated based on velocity)
6. Sprint planning docs (all sprints)
7. Sprint retrospectives with action items
8. Burndown charts
