### What Makes a Great Final Presentation:

**Not this**: "I built an event system. It has login and search. The end."

**This**: "College students miss 70% of campus events because information is scattered. I've personally missed job fairs, free food, and networking events. So I built a centralized event discovery system that solves this. Let me show you... [demo]... Building this taught me that design is 10% of the work and implementation is 90%. I underestimated story points every sprint until Sprint 3. But I learned. That's the point."

**You're not showing me code. You're showing me growth.**
