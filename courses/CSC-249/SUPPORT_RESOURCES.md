## 9. Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I use AI to do my homework?**
A: Yes! AI collaboration is REQUIRED. But you must understand and document it in prompts.txt. Code you can't explain = plagiarism.

**Q: How much help is "too much help" in Discord?**
A: Share concepts, debugging strategies, and hints. Don't share complete solutions. "Try checking if the pointer is null" ‚úì vs "Here's my whole linked list code" ‚úó

**Q: I'm falling behind. What do I do?**
A: Email instructor immediately. Options: extension, reduced scope, modified deadlines. Communication = solutions.

**Q: The sprint process feels like busywork. Do I have to do it?**
A: Yes. It's 40% of your grade and teaches professional practices. By Sprint 3, you'll see why it works.

**Q: Can I switch from C++ to Python mid-semester?**
A: Yes, but ideally decide by Week 3. Switching later means redoing earlier assignments.

**Q: I have a disability accommodation. How do I use it?**
A: Register with Disability Services ‚Üí Get accommodation letter ‚Üí Meet with instructor to discuss implementation.
